Simple Omelette recipe.

An easy and quick dish for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfiction, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preperation time.

  • Total: Approcimately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: Approcimately 5 minutes
  • Cooking: Approcimately 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese,diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Bead the egg: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinck of salt and pepper until thye are well mixed. You can add atablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick fying pan over medium heat and a butter.
  3. Cook the omeletteOnce The butter is melted and bubbling, pour int the eggs. Telt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surace.
  4. Add fillings (optional)When the eggs beginto set at the edgese but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinke your chosen fillingover one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and server As the omeletter continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filling. Lit it cook for another minute, then slid it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
